Welcome To the Fantasy Feline League

The inaugural season kicks off October 7th.

The Rules:



  1. Season Rruns from 10/7 to 12/01
  2. When selecting your draft picks, you may NOT select your own cat.
  3. Snake Draft, order chosen at random.
  4. You may NOT select a cat you live with
  5. By entering the draft, you are committing to updating Tevvy on your cats behavior even when it results in another team getting points, as well as not intentionally modifying their behavior
